📋 Make.com setup guide
Step-by-step — takes about 10 minutes
▼
Step 1 — Create a free Make.com account
  1. Go to make.com and click Get started for free
  2. Sign up with Google or email → verify your email
  3. You get 1,000 free operations/month — more than enough for daily digests
Step 2 — Create a new scenario
  1. In the dashboard, click the big + button or "Create a new scenario"
  2. You'll see a blank canvas with a circle — this is where you'll chain your modules
Step 3 — Add Module 1: Webhook (the trigger)
  1. Click the + circle on the canvas
  2. Search for Webhook → click Custom webhook
  3. Click Add → name it MARK Weekly Digest → click Save
  4. Copy the webhook URL that appears — it looks like:
    https://hook.make.com/abc123def456ghi789
  5. Click OK
  6. Paste that URL into the Webhook URL field above on this page and click Save
Step 4 — Test the webhook (important — do this before adding more modules)
  1. In Make, click "Run once" at the bottom of the screen
  2. Come back to this page, fill in your email and a date range, then click Send digest to my inbox
  3. Go back to Make — you should see a green bubble on the Webhook module
  4. Click that bubble to confirm Make received your data — you'll see all the stats fields listed
⚠️ Don't skip this test. Make.com needs to receive real data once before you can map fields in the next steps.
Step 5 — Add Module 2: Parse JSON
  1. Click the + to the right of your Webhook module
  2. Search JSON → click Parse JSON
  3. In the JSON string field, click inside it → a panel opens on the right → click stats_json under the Webhook section
    The field should show: {{"{{"}}1.stats_json{{"}}"}}
  4. For Data structure, click Generate → paste this sample → click Save:
{
  "weekStart": "2026-06-14",
  "weekEnd": "2026-06-20",
  "totalTrades": 7,
  "netPnl": 1188.10,
  "winRate": 85.71,
  "wins": 6,
  "losses": 1,
  "breakevens": 0,
  "profitFactor": 4.2,
  "bestTrade": { "symbol": "GBPJPY", "pnl": 305.32 },
  "worstTrade": { "symbol": "EURUSD", "pnl": -88.00 }
}
Click OK when done.
Step 6 — Add Module 3: Send Email via Gmail
  1. Click + next to Parse JSON → search Gmail → click Send an Email
    (If you don't use Gmail, search Email → Send an email instead)
  2. Connection: Click Add → sign in to your Gmail → allow Make access
  3. To: Click inside the field → right panel opens → click email under the Webhook section
  4. Subject: Type: Your MARK. Weekly Digest — {{"{{"}}2.weekStart{{"}}"}} to {{"{{"}}2.weekEnd{{"}}"}}
  5. Content type: Select HTML
  6. Body: Paste the HTML template below

Step 7 — Activate and test end-to-end
  1. Click Save (floppy disk icon at the bottom)
  2. Toggle the ON/OFF switch to ON
  3. Come back here, fill in your email and date range, and click Send digest to my inbox
  4. Check your inbox — and your Spam / Promotions tab if you don't see it within 2 minutes
Troubleshooting
Webhook shows no data Click "Run once" in Make before sending from this page
Fields show literally {{2.netPnl}} You typed them instead of using the field picker — re-map each field using the right-side panel
Gmail connection failed Remove and re-add the connection — make sure you tick all permission checkboxes Google shows
Email goes to spam Add Make's sender address to your contacts, or check the Promotions tab in Gmail
